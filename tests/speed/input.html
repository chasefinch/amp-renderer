      Mandarin Oriental Atlanta

      • Website
      • Directions
      Mandarin Oriental Atlanta welcomes two pets up to 25 lbs for an additional fee of $100 per pet, per stay. Pet-friendly rooms are limited. Guests traveling with pets must call 877-411-3436 prior to booking to verify availability of a pet friendly room or risk not being able to check in with a pet and/or incurring any associated cancellation penalties.

      Description

      The Mansion on Peachtree is designed by noted American architect Robert A.M. Stern. Towering high in the picturesque Atlanta skyline in the citys most affluent neighborhood of Buckhead.The slender 42-story property is crafted from classically inspired limestone and cast stone for dramatic effect. The property amenities include a dining room a fitness center a swimming pool room service and high-speed Internet. The hotel also offers wheelchair access a business center meeting rooms and interior corridors. Each and every guest of this hotel will experience unrivaled luxury breathtaking views and unparalleled personal service.The property combines an intimate 127-room ultra-luxury hotel with 45 sumptuous residences.

      Health & Safety

      Safe Stay: Mandarin Oriental Atlanta has enhanced cleaning and safety measures in place. Hand sanitizer is provided to guests, and rooms are disinfected between each stay. Social distancing measures are in place, as well as physical barriers between staff and guests. Contactless check-in and check-out are available.
